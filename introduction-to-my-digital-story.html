Welcome to my personal archive of lurking/learning through my mobile phone. But before we continue, I want to ask you all: 

What does lurking mean for you? 

I want to incorporate a live text box where site users can submit their answers and ideally it could be collected somewhere? 

"Lurking in the Piggys Lot Pond" by dsgetch is licensed under CC BY 2.0

After transitioning to the role of "lurker" on my social media accounts, I noticed how social media became a space for me to gather more information about various different aspects of my life and (of others). But more importantly, I started to notice how I began to rely on my social media accounts to save things. Facebook (now Meta) was amongst the first to introduce the "saved" feature. Pretty soon, I had a huge collection of news articles, pdfs, images, URLs, and Facebook posts that I could access anytime I wanted...considering I don't get de-platformed. By the end of 2017, I had deactivated my Facebook, and switched over to Instagram, but my archive is still available and accessible to me. 

This space is for exploring how lurking and archiving have reoriented the subject-position of the 'curious user' on social media.
