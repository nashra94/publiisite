The Lurker 

What does it mean to do research online on social media?

This page will capture glimpses into the life of an lurker/internet researcher: En Em

This project is an auto-ethnographic experiment where I will display how studying Twitter has influenced me as scholar and as a social media user. The intention is to provide an interactive story/timeline where users can 'become' En Em and learn about the positive and negatives of social media, through an embodied, affective experience. 

My goal is to produce an interactive game via Twine which allows players to experience how radicalization takes place in online spaces, and also come away from critical insights into how to combat it.
